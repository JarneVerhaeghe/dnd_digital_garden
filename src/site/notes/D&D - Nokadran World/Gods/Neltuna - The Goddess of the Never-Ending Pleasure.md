# Neltuna - The Goddess of the Never-Ending Pleasure
## Background
Neltuna loves all forms of pleasure; from entertainment, music, lust, to torture and even pleasure gained from war. As long as there is pleasure, she follows. She is one of the Neutral Ones, as she deemed that choosing a side in the Ascension war was too boring and would limit her to what she truly wanted. Instead, she watched the ongoing suffering and even instigated some conflicts on both sides, just to enjoy it. Because of this, Neltuna also represents free choice and doing whatever your heart desires. Due to her trickery and never-ending quest for pleasure, she somehow managed to survive the Ascension Wars unscathed. She is often depicted as a laughing lustful woman with a whip and a lyre in battle armour, showing all sides of her pleasure-seeking personality. Many bards and free-willed spirits worship her.
