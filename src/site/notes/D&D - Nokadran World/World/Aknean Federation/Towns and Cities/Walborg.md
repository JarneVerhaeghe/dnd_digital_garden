# Walborg
## Description
A bordering city of the Aknean Federation. As the city is situated on the High Road it is an important city for the Aknean Federation, used for diplomatic relationships with the Undying Empire and official trade. The Westgate is already the Undying information. The city is situated in between two mountains and serves as a gate. Nicknamed: The Gate Between The Mountains.
