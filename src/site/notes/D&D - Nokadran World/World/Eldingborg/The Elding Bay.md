# The Elding Bay
## Background
This body of water is the large bay around Eldingborg and completely under the governance of Eldingborg. Further to the east you have The Silent Reefs where the [[D&D - Nokadran World/World/The Sovereignty of Thalassa/The Sovereignty of Thalassa\|The Sovereignty of Thalassa]] is located.
