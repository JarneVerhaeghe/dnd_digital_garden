# The Noble  Council
   - **Composition**: The Noble Council consists of high-ranking Ascended Nobles who oversee various aspects of the kingdom, such as trade, justice, and defense. They are appointed by the king and serve at his pleasure.
   - **Responsibilities**: The council manages the day-to-day affairs of the kingdom, ensuring that the king's vision is carried out. They also act as a judicial body, resolving disputes and enforcing the laws of the land.
