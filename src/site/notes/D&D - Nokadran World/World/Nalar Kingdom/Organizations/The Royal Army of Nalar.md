# The Royal Army of Nalar
## In Short
   - **Role**: The Royal Army is the military arm of the kingdom, tasked with protecting its borders, enforcing the law, and maintaining internal order. They are led by high-ranking Nobles who report directly to the king.
   - **Structure**: The army is organized into various divisions, each with specialized roles such as border patrol, internal security, and elite guard units that protect the king and the court.
