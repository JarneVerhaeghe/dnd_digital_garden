# Holy Church of Aena and Emos
## Background
This is the religious organization of the Nokari Coasts. These govern the political, religious, and economic aspects of the country together with [[D&D - Nokadran World/World/Nokari Coasts/Organizations/The Radiant Merchant Guild\|The Radiant Merchant Guild]] and [[D&D - Nokadran World/World/Nokari Coasts/Organizations/The Tribunal of Balance\|The Tribunal of Balance]]. The ranks are as follows (in ascending order): Novice, Priest, Cleric, High Priest, Arch Paladin/Arch Cleric, Cardinal, Pontifex. With the Pontifex being the leader of the Nokari Coasts and the church.
