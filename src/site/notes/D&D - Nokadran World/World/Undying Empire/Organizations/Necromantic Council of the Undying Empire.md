# Necromantic Council of the Undying Empire
## Background
The upper magical council led by the Emperor [[DnD Campaign 1 - happy adventures/Personages/BIG NPCS/Auron Denholm\|Auron Denholm]]. The previous advisor was [[DnD Campaign 1 - happy adventures/Personages/BEG/Arvin Forgemaul\|Arvin Forgemaul]] however toppled 40 years ago.
